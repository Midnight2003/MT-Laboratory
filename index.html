How It Works

Getting your e-waste recycled responsibly is simple — four easy steps from request to certificate.

01

Request a Pickup

Fill out the form below or call us. Tell us what devices you have and where you're located.

02

We Schedule & Collect

Our trained team arrives at your location on your chosen date and collects all e-waste items.

03

Certified Processing

Items are sorted, processed, and recycled at our DENR-accredited facility following strict protocols.

04

Receive Certificate

You receive a Certificate of Recycling and/or Data Destruction — full legal compliance covered.

For over two decades, Jairapel International Corp. has been at the forefront of responsible electronics waste management in the Philippines — serving government agencies, multinational corporations, hospitals, schools, and households alike.

Our DENR-accredited facility in Parañaque City processes thousands of metric tons of electronic waste annually, recovering valuable materials while preventing hazardous substances from entering the environment.
